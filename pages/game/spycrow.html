Spycrow

Spycrow is a stealth game with a top-down camera where the player takes on the role of a crow that must collect all the pumpkins in the farm.

Roles:  Game Designer, Game Developer

Tools:  Unity

Platform:  PC

What I did: 

  1. Designed-developed the entire game from scratch;

What I learned: 

  1. Random grid generation;
  2. Mesh creation from scratch;
  3. Usage of the Singleton Pattern;
  4. Keeping a small scope with clear goals is better than trying to develop all at once;
  5. Design game mechanics with small learning curve and at the same time challenging enough.
